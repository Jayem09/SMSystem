# Pages Structure

This folder follows a domain-based organization:

- `auth/` - Authentication pages (Login, Register)
- `admin/` - Admin-only pages (Staff, Branches, Settings, etc.)
- `inventory/` - Inventory management (Products, Categories, Brands, Suppliers)
- `sales/` - Sales operations (POS, Orders, Customers, Expenses)
- `reports/` - Reporting and analytics (Dashboard, Analytics)

**Migration:** Existing pages remain at root until refactored. New pages go into appropriate subfolder.
